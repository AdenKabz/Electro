Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to your questions about our electricity services.

1. How can I sign up for your services?

Signing up is easy! Simply visit our website, click on the "Sign Up" button, and follow the on-screen instructions. You'll need to provide your personal information and choose a suitable plan.

2. What are the different payment options available?

We offer a variety of payment options for your convenience, including online payments, credit/debit card, and automatic bank withdrawals. You can manage your payments through your online account.

3. How can I track my electricity usage?

With our smart metering system, you can easily monitor your electricity consumption in real-time through your online account. You'll have access to detailed usage reports and can identify areas where you can conserve energy.

4. What happens if there's a power outage?

We work diligently to maintain a reliable power supply. However, in the event of a power outage, you can report the issue by contacting our customer support team. We will dispatch a technician as soon as possible to restore power.

5. How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team by phone, email, or through our online chat feature. Our representatives are available to assist you 24/7.
